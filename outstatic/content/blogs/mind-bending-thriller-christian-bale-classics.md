### [The Prestige (2006)](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0482571/)

Director: Christopher Nolan

Starring: Hugh Jackman, Christian Bale, Scarlett Johansson, Michael Caine

Genre: Mystery/Thriller

Ratings: IMDb: 8.5/10; Rotten Tomatoes: 76%

---

### We Watched It

The Prestige takes you into a dark, intriguing world of rivalry, obsession, and secrets. When you think you’ve figured it out, the film reveals another layer of mystery. Jackman and Bale shine as rival magicians, each pushing the limits of their craft in a relentless battle for supremacy. The movie’s carefully crafted cinematography and intricate plot keep you engaged right up to the final twist. And, for an extra surprise, keep an eye out for a unique portrayal of Nikola Tesla. If you enjoyed the mind-bending twists of *Fight Club* or *Shutter Island*, this film is a must-watch.

### Behind The Scenes

David Bowie, who played the elusive inventor Nikola Tesla, initially declined the role. Nolan flew to Bowie’s home to convince him to join the cast, stating that no one else could play Tesla. Bowie finally agreed, and his performance became one of the film’s unforgettable highlights.

### 3 Christian Bale Movies

[Ford v Ferrari (2019)](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1950186/) – This high-octane biographical drama pits Ford against Ferrari in a gripping story of innovation and determination. Christian Bale plays Ken Miles, a fearless driver who teams up with visionary car designer Carroll Shelby to take on the impossible at the 24 Hours of Le Mans. It's an exhilarating underdog story with action-packed racing scenes and deep emotional moments—perfect for fans of both speed and heart.

[American Hustle (2013)](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1800241/?ref_=fn_al_tt_1) – In this slick crime drama, Bale transforms into a con artist roped into a sting operation by the FBI to bring down corrupt politicians. Set in the glitzy, chaotic world of the late 1970s, the film delivers humor, style, and powerful performances. If you like stories of cons, scandals, and larger-than-life characters, American Hustle will keep you entertained from start to finish.

[American Psycho (2000)](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0144084/?ref_=nv_sr_srsg_0_tt_8_nm_0_in_0_q_american%2520psy): Bale’s portrayal of Patrick Bateman, a wealthy investment banker with a sinister secret, is both iconic and deeply unsettling. The film’s sharp social commentary on materialism and excess in 1980s New York is wrapped in a chilling psychological thriller. It’s perfect for fans of dark satire and psychological horror, offering both disturbing tension and biting humor.
